Flexible Batches


Our training institute offers flexible batches to accommodate the diverse schedules of our students, allowing them to pursue their education at their convenience. With morning, afternoon, and evening sessions, our flexible batches cater to individuals with varying work commitments and personal obligations. Students have the option to choose from weekend-only, weekday-only, or a combination of both, providing a customized learning experience that fits their lifestyle. Our flexible batch schedules enable working professionals to upskill or reskill without interrupting their current job responsibilities, promoting a seamless integration of education and career development. Small class sizes in each batch ensure personalized attention from our experienced trainers, fostering a conducive learning environment for all participants. We understand that life can be unpredictable, so our flexible batches come with the option for students to attend makeup classes or switch between batches when needed. Online and offline learning options are available within our flexible batches, giving students the choice to attend classes in-person or remotely based on their preferences or circumstances. Whether you're a full-time student, a working professional, or someone with other commitments, our flexible batches provide the adaptability needed to pursue your educational goals. Our institute's commitment to accessibility is reflected in our flexible batch schedules, ensuring that education remains within reach for everyone, regardless of their daily routine.

100% Placement Assurance

Our training institute takes pride in offering a comprehensive placement assurance program to support our students in their career journey. We have established strong connections with a network of reputable companies and industry partners, enhancing the opportunities for our students to secure promising job placements. Our placement assurance program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills, knowledge, and confidence to excel in interviews and showcase their abilities to potential employers. A dedicated placement cell works tirelessly to bridge the gap between our students and prospective employers, facilitating recruitment drives, job fairs, and networking events. Mock interviews, resume workshops, and career counseling sessions are integral components of our placement assurance program, preparing students for the challenges of the competitive job market. We maintain a current and up-to-date database of job openings, ensuring that our students have access to a wide range of employment opportunities across various industries.
